# Attention Capital Balance Sheet

## Purpose
Calculate your attention net worth. Track assets (what compounds) vs liabilities (what depletes).

## Template

### ASSETS (Attention that Compounds)
**Deep Work Blocks:**
- [ ] Monday: ___ hours
- [ ] Tuesday: ___ hours  
- [ ] [etc.]
- **Weekly Total:** ___ hours

**DMN Sessions:**
- [ ] Sessions completed: ___
- [ ] Duration: ___ minutes each
- **Weekly Total:** ___ minutes

**Strategic Conversations:**
- [ ] Strangers engaged: ___
- [ ] Deep 1-on-1s: ___

**Learning/Exploration:**
- [ ] Books/articles (substantial): ___
- [ ] New environments: ___

**TOTAL ASSETS:** ___ hours of compounding attention

---

### LIABILITIES (Attention Depleting)
**Shallow Work:**
- [ ] Meetings (no clear outcome): ___ hours
- [ ] Email/Slack (reactive): ___ hours  

**Distraction:**
- [ ] Social media scrolling: ___ hours
- [ ] News/YouTube rabbit holes: ___ hours

**Fragile Middle Activities:**
- [ ] Neither deep focus nor rest: ___ hours

**TOTAL LIABILITIES:** ___ hours of depleted attention

---

### NET WORTH
**Assets - Liabilities = ___ hours**

**Target:** Net positive (more compounding than depleting)

**Red Flag:** Net negative 2 weeks in a row = barbell audit needed
