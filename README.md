In Mongolia's silence, I discovered three foundational patterns required to maintain agency:

### 1. üßπ Garbage Collect Input

### 2. üß† Mind Wandering Loop
**Activate your Default Mode Network (DMN) for original insight generation.**

The train station moment in Mongolia‚Äîfinding the impossible border crossing‚Äîthat was DMN at work.

### 3. üí° Reflection Loop
**Use AI as a mirror, not a prescription machine.**
Action ‚Üí Feedback ‚Üí Correction. Wander ‚Üí Capture ‚Üí AI feedback to structure thoughts and identify blind spots. This creates compounding cognitive growth.

Plus frameworks for adaptive focus, signal clarity, and conscious elimination.

## üöÄ Your First Move
